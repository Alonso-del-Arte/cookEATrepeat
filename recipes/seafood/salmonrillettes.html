Everything is better when it is wetter.
Straight from the sea!

Algorithm:

  1. Cut the smoked salmon into a 1/4" dice. Let rest at room temperature.
  2. Place the salmon fillet in a glass baking dish. Sprinkle each side with 1 tbs of Pernod, 1 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp white pepper.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for @1 hour (flip half way).
  4. Steam salmon and gently until medium-rare, about 8 minutes.
  5. Brown shallots in butter, with 1/4 tsp salt, stirring, until softened, about 4-5 minutes. Don't allow them to brown.
  6. In a small bowl, stir the remaining 7 tbs butter until smooth and creamy. Add creme fraiche and stir to combine. Set aside.
  7. In a large bowl, combine steamed salmon, diced smoked salmon, sauteed shallots, lemon juice, olive oil, egg yolks, and chives.
  8. Season with salt and white pepper. Fold in the butter mixture and combine well.
  9. Transfer salmon mixture to ramekins, canning jars, bain, etc. leaving a half inch of space at the top of each.
  10. Refrigerate 30 minutes.
  11. Meanwhile, heat clarified butter over medium heat until liquid.
  12. Pour a half inch layer of butter over each pot of salmon and return to the refrigerator to solidify.
  13. To serve, remove and discard the butter seal.
  14. Serve spread on toasted baguette, crackers, cucumber slices, or spoons.

Pan Fry tips:

  1. Oil pan and heat over med-high heat, till oil shimmers.
  2. Place wontons in pan, just enough room that they wont stick to eachother.
  3. Sear for 5-6mins, adjusting heat to not burn, but still sizzling.
  4. Deglaze with @1/4 cup of water (pour it around the edges).
  5. Cover and steam for @2mins till no water remains.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. The wontons should pop from the bottom of the pan with a nice golden-brown base.

Variables:

  • 1 lb center-cut wild-caught salmon, skin and pinbones removed, trimmed and dark flesh removed
  • 2 tbs Pernod
  • 8 tbs unsalted butter
  • 1/2 c shallots, minced
  • 1 tbs creme fraiche
  • 8 oz un-sliced, chilled smoked salmon
  • 2.5 tbs lemon juice
  • 1 tbs high-quality extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tbs fresh chives, finely chopped, optional
  • salt and white pepper
  • clarified butter, for sealing
