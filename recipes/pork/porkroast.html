This Little Piggy went to market...

Algorithm:

  1. Cube pork into 2" cubes, trimming excess fat. Sprinkle generously with salt and let rest at room temperature.
  2. Sear pork in hot canola oil till crispy edges. Set aside.
  3. Add leeks, onion and garlic, 1/2 the carrots, and 1/2 the celery to slow cooker. Place pork atop. Pour stock till pork is nearly covered.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 5-6 hours. Till fork tender.
  5. Remove your pork loin from your crock pot, place in a heat proof dish. Strain broth.
  6. Boil remaining carrots and celery in broth till desired tenderness.
  7. Make slurry with 1 tablespoon of cornstarch and water. Stir into boiling broth, repeat till desired viscosity.
  8. Remove from heat and add pork back to gravy and vegetables.
  9. Serve atop mashed potatoes.

Variables:

  • 1 3-4 lb pork shoulder roast, boneless
  • 2 large leeks, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 6 large carrots, chopped
  • 6 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed
  • cornstarch
