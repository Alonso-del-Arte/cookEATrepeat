This Little Piggy went to market...

Algorithm:

  1. Rinse and dry Pork Shoulder.
  2. Massage the seasonings into the meat.
  3. Place in slow cooker fat side up, and top with the herbs, peppers, onions and garlic.
  4. Cook on low for 10hrs. Finished when the meat shreds easily with a fork.
  5. Remove and shred with a fork in caserole dish. Skim the fat from the juice, and set aside the remaining liquid.
  6. Reduce liquid down to @2 cups. Pour half over shredded pork.
  7. Place in oven (broil) @425°F for 15mins till crispy bits form.
  8. Remove from oven and pour remaining juices over shredded pork.
  9. Serve with whatever suits your fancy.

Variables:

  • 3-4 pounds of pork shoulder (butt)
  • 1 yellow onion diced (1/2 inch)
  • 2 peeled carrots
  • 4 large garlic cloves, pressed
  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 1 green/red pepper
  • 2 pieces bacon
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 orange
  • 4 sprigs of thyme
  • 1 tablespoons cumin
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoons oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
