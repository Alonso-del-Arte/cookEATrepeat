This Little Piggy went to market...

Algorithm:

  1. Salt Cabbage and drain excess water
  2. Mix all ingredients
  3. Place @1 teaspoon of mixture in wonton
  4. Wrap wonton however your heart suits (practice will make it easier)
  5. Cook: Pan fry, Steam, etc.

Pan Fry tips:

  1. Oil pan and heat over med-high heat, till oil shimmers.
  2. Place wontons in pan, just enough room that they wont stick to eachother.
  3. Sear for 5-6mins, adjusting heat to not burn, but still sizzling.
  4. Deglaze with @1/4 cup of water (pour it around the edges).
  5. Cover and steam for @2mins till no water remains.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. The wontons should pop from the bottom of the pan with a nice golden-brown base.

Variables:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 package wonton wrappers
  • 1 pund ground pork
  • 3 shitake mushrooms
  • 1 cup scallions
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ginger
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tablesppons sesame oil
  • salt and pepper
