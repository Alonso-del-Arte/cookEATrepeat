Eating breads and cakes. Getting all fat and sassy!

Algorithm:

  1. Destem and wash blueberries.
  2. Place 2 cups blueberries in pot with the water, and half of the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Bring to a boil, add thyme.
  4. Reduce liquid down to a blueberry gravy, stirring frequently and breaking some in the process.
  5. Add remaining fresh blueberries and a splash of lemon juice after you remove from the heat.
  6. Grease pie tin, and line with pie pastry.
  7. Egg wash bottom pie pastry.
  8. Pour blueberry gravy into the pie tin.
  9. Top with top pie pastry and egg wash, top with dots of butter.
  10. Bake for @45mins at 375°F.

Variables:

  • 5 cups blueberries
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 splash of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup water (or champagne)
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme (or rosemary)
  • 1 9" Pie Pastry
