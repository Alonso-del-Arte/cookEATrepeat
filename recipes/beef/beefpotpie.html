Beef! It's whats for dinner!

Algorithm:

  1. Cube short ribs
  2. Cook vegetables in a little chicken broth till al dente (reserve peas as they will cook enough in the pot pie).
  3. Mix vegetables, meat and gravy together.
  4. Grease large muffin tin.
  5. Line each with pastry dough, and egg wash bottoms.
  6. Fill with short rib gravy mixture.
  7. Top with pastry dough, and egg wash.
  8. Bake at 375°F for @45mins.

Variables:

  • Beef Short Ribs Recipe
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 cup cubed carrots
  • 1 cup cubed celery
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1 cup cubed potatoes (roasted)
  • 1 package pastry dough
  • 1 egg (beaten to wash dough)
