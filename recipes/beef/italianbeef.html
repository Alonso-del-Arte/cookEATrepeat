Beef! It's whats for dinner!

Algorithm:

  1. Cut chuck roast into 3" cubes, trim large chunks of fat. Sprinkle generously with salt and let rest at room temperature.
  2. Heat canola oil till shimmering in frying pan to sear beef in.
  3. Sear all sides of beef till crispy edges form and a nice seared brown exterior is on at least half the pieces. Add oil as needed.
  4. When immediatly finished searing, deglaze the pan with the onions and 1/2 cup of beef stock.
  5. Scrape pan for fried bits and fat remnants.
  6. Dump contents of frying pan into slow cooker with remaining onions, thyme, rosemary, garlic, 1/2 the oregano and bay leafs.
  7. Place beef atop, cover with remaining oregano. Pour beef stock, leaving a bit of the beef exposed.
  8. Cover, and cook on Low for 10 to 12 hours. Beef will shred with a fork.
  9. Shred with fork.
  10. Serve in a hoagie with mustard and peppers.

Variables:

  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 1/4 cup dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 bay leaf
  • 4 pound chuck roast
  • salt and pepper
