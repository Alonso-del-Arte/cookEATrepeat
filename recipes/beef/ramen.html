Beef! It's whats for dinner!

Algorithm:

  1. Season steak with salt liberally, and let rest at room temperature for 20mins.
  2. Heat canola oil in a pan till shimmering (realy hot) but not smoking.
  3. Sear the meat to desired temperature, set aside.
  4. Reduce to medium heat, and deglaze the pot with the onions and a little oil. Stir till all the burnt bits are loose from the bottom of the pot.
  5. Add ginger, garlic, celery and mushrooms. Cook till celery is barely crunchy.
  6. Add soup bones, and soy sauce. Simmmer for 1-2hrs till bone marrow is disolved.
  7. Remove the bones and strain the broth.
  8. Cook vegetables for broth to desired tenderness.
  9. Cook noodles.
  10. Slice steak to desired thickness.
  11. Combine all prepared ingredients in a soup bowl.
  12. Serve with hardboiled egg, fresh jalapenos and parsley.

Variables:

  • 1 pound filet mignon or teres major steaks
  • 1 pound beef soup bones
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium ginger root
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 14oz mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon oriental 5 spice (optional)
  • 6 cups water with appropriate bouillon
  • Hard boiled eggs
  • Jalapeno peppers
  • Parsley
  • *extra vegetables in broth*
