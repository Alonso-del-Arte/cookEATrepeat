Beef! It's whats for dinner!

Algorithm:

  1. Peel and cut potoates. Let rest in cold water with 3 sprigs of rosemary for @2hrs.
  2. Cube short ribs
  3. Add gravy to sauce pan, bring to gentle simmer.
  4. Add mushrooms to simmering gravy. Cook till tender.
  5. Bring potatoes and salted water to boil. Put remaining rosemary sprigs in. Boil till potatoes are tender.
  6. Remove rosemary sprigs. Add salt, milk and butter to potatoes. Mash to desired texture.
  7. Combine and serve.

Variables:

  • Beef Short Ribs Recipe
  • 4 cups green vegetable
  • 6 medium potoates
  • 6 sprigs rosemary
  • 2 cups milk
  • Butter
  • Salt Pepper
