Beef! It's whats for dinner!

Algorithm:

  1. Braise Beef in Dutch Oven. Set aside as it is finished.
  2. Deglaze pan with onions and a little beef broth.
  3. Add celery, carrots, garlic, beef broth and bay leafs.
  4. Bring to boil and reduce to a simmer.
  5. Add beef and beef bones (optional), simmer for 1 - 2 hrs.
  6. Strain broth, remove beef and return broth back to pot.
  7. Cook carrots, celery indepently to desired texture.
  8. Cube beef and return back to broth. Add cooked vegetables and tomatoes.
  9. Add barely and bring to boil, reduce to simmer for @45mins till barely is tender.
  10. Make corn starch slurry 1 tblspn at a time and add till desired viscosity.

Variables:

  • 1-2 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 4 carrots (2 for broth, 2 for soup)
  • 4 stalks celery (broth and soup)
  • 1 package mushrooms
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 1/2 cups pearl barley
  • 10 cups water with appropriate beef bouillon
  • 2 bay leafs
  • @2 tablespoons corn startch
